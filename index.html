8 Reasons

Why Every Realtor Needs
Their Own Website

Your brokerage's site is great for them. This is great for you.

01

🏡 You Own Your Brand — Not Your Brokerage

When you rely solely on your company's website, you're building their brand, not yours. If you switch brokerages — and most agents do — you lose your entire online presence overnight. A personal site travels with you, carrying your reviews, your listings, and your story no matter where your career takes you.

02

🌟 Stand Out in a Crowded Market

Most realtors share the exact same generic company page with hundreds of other agents. A personal website lets you showcase your personality, your niche, your client testimonials, and your story — giving buyers and sellers a clear, compelling reason to choose you specifically over everyone else in the office.

03

📥 Capture & Own Your Own Leads

Leads generated on your brokerage's website go to the company first — not directly to you. With your own personal site, every inquiry, every contact form submission, and every phone call comes straight to your inbox. No middleman. No lost opportunities. No commission splits on digital leads.

04

🔍 Build a Long-Term Google Presence

A personal website you invest in today keeps ranking on Google for years. Clients searching "realtor in [your city]" or "[your name] real estate" can find you — not just your company's generic page. Over time, your SEO compounds, making every dollar you spend now worth more and more.

05

⭐ Showcase Your Reviews & Testimonials

You've worked hard to earn those glowing five-star reviews. Your brokerage's site won't spotlight your individual reputation — but your personal site will. Feature every testimonial front and center, build social proof that converts, and let your clients' words do the selling for you.

06

🏠 Market Your Listings Your Way

Feature your active listings with full-screen photography, embedded video tours, neighborhood guides, and market stats — all on a page that reflects your professionalism. No competing for space with other agents, no corporate template restrictions. Your listings deserve your presentation.

07

🔄 Stay in Control If You Move Brokerages

The real estate industry sees a lot of movement between firms. Agents who've built their own personal websites never start from zero when they make a switch. Your domain stays the same, your Google ranking stays intact, your reviews stay visible, and your clients can always find you.

08

💼 Look More Professional to High-Value Clients

Luxury buyers and serious sellers research their agent before they ever pick up the phone. A polished, professional personal website signals credibility, commitment, and attention to detail — the exact qualities that win six-figure listing appointments and high-net-worth buyers.

How It Works

Five steps from first message to a live website — with you in control the whole way.

1
Step One

Contact — Tell Us What You Want

Reach out via our contact form or email. Tell us about yourself — your name, your market, your style, what you want the site to accomplish. This is completely free with zero obligation. We'll ask a few questions to understand your vision before we do anything.

2
Step Two

Prototype — We Build a Working Draft

We get to work and build you a fully working prototype of your website — not a wireframe or mockup, but a real, clickable site based on everything you told us. You'll be able to see exactly how your finished site will look and feel before any money changes hands.

3
Step Three

Review / Edit — Your Feedback, Our Revisions

We share the prototype with you and you review every page. Want the colors adjusted? A section moved? Different copy? Tell us. We go back and forth until the site looks and feels exactly how you envisioned it. This is your website — we don't finalize anything until you're happy.

4
Step Four

Finalize — We Polish Every Detail

Once you've approved the design, we do a full quality pass — checking every link, every image, every form, and every page on desktop and mobile. We make sure the site is fast, clean, and looks exactly right. You get one final sign-off before we move to launch.

5
Step Five

Payment + Add-Ons — You Pay, We Launch

Only at this point — after you've seen and approved your finished site — do you pay the flat $75 fee. You can also choose from optional add-ons (see pricing below). Once payment is received, your site goes live. That's it.
